Fans

Fans are used in PCs to increase air flow to make the cooling better. Most PCs have some fans built in to the case, GPU, and coolers. However, adding extra fans to the PC can have some benefits.

Adding fans to your PC can improve the cooling by moving hot air away from the main components. Adding fans can improve the performance of the CPU, hard drives, and GPU. Extra fans can be controlled using a fan controller or a fan hub splitter that can plug into the motherboard. If you choose to add RGB fans, then the overall asthetic of the PC can also be improved. Some RGB fans can also be programmed to change colors based on the input or output volume levels from the PC.
